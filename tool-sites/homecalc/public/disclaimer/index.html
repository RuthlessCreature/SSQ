Disclaimer

HomeCalc Tools is for planning and educational use. Calculator outputs are estimates based on the numbers you enter and simplified assumptions.

Material coverage, waste, price, surface condition, installation method, and local product availability can change real project requirements. Confirm final quantities with a qualified professional or supplier before purchasing.

Examples on this site use typical planning assumptions for common residential projects. They are not quotes, guarantees, building-code advice, or product-specific instructions.

Estimate limitations

Calculator results can be wrong if measurements are incomplete, if product labels use different coverage assumptions, if the room is irregular, or if the project has hidden preparation needs. Paint may need primer, tile may need waterproofing and trim, flooring may need subfloor work, and curtains may need lining, hardware, or pattern matching.

Prices entered into the calculators are examples or user inputs. They do not include taxes, delivery, labor, disposal, tools, permit fees, store minimums, or local market changes unless you add those costs separately.

No professional advice

The site does not provide architectural, engineering, code, structural, electrical, plumbing, waterproofing, accessibility, or safety advice. Use qualified local professionals and manufacturer instructions for final decisions.

Use the calculators as a first-pass planning aid. Before buying, verify measurements, product coverage, batch or dye lot, installation method, and return policy.
