Privacy Policy

HomeCalc Tools runs calculator logic in the browser. The calculators do not require an account, and the site does not ask you to submit your name, address, phone number, or project photos to use the tools.

Measurements you type into the calculators are processed in your browser for the purpose of showing an estimate. The site is designed so ordinary calculator inputs do not need to be sent to a HomeCalc Tools application server.

Information you choose to send

If you contact the site by email, the message may include your email address, page URL, input values, screenshots, or other details you decide to include. That information is used to respond, troubleshoot, correct content, or evaluate requested improvements.

Analytics and ads

The site may use analytics to understand aggregate page traffic, errors, device types, and popular pages. The site may also display advertising through Google AdSense or similar partners. Advertising partners may use cookies, device identifiers, or similar technologies according to their own policies.

Ad partners may use information from your visit to serve and measure ads. You can control cookies and tracking through your browser settings and through any privacy controls offered by the advertising provider.

Result cards and sharing

Some tools can create a result card image in your browser or copy the current page link. Downloaded result cards are generated locally by your browser. If you share a result image or link with another service, that service handles the shared content under its own policy.

Data retention

HomeCalc Tools does not maintain user accounts or saved project histories. Email records may be retained as needed for support, abuse prevention, legal compliance, or business records.
