Tile guide

Bathroom tile buying checklist.

Bathrooms often have more cuts than open rooms. Plan tile, trim, grout, waterproofing, and spare pieces together.

Checklist

  • Floor and wall square footage measured separately.
  • Waste allowance set to at least 10-15%.
  • Trim pieces, edging, thresholds, and niche tile counted.
  • Grout color, spacers, waterproofing, and setting materials selected.
  • One spare box stored for future repair if possible.
