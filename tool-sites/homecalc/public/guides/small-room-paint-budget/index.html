Paint guide

Small room paint budget basics.

A small room can still need more paint than expected when it has high walls, dark color changes, or rough surfaces.

Fast estimate

A 10 ft by 10 ft room with 8 ft walls has around 320 square feet of wall area before subtracting openings. Two coats means planning for roughly 500-600 square feet of coverage after doors and windows.

Budget drivers

  • Paint quality and finish.
  • Primer needs.
  • Brushes, rollers, tape, drop cloths, and patching supplies.
